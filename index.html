Places Must Visit

la sagrada familia(spain-Europe)

i have visited the place in december 2019

It is definitely worth visiting the Sagrada Familia towers for a breathtaking view of both the Basilica and Barcelona. The four towers of the Nativity are the oldest, the only ones Gaudi knew of in his lifetime. The four towers of the Passion are the most recent. On each side, a walkway connects the central towers.Is Sagrada Familia a good area to stay? Image result for sagrada familia reviews La Sagrada Familia is truly one of the most unique experiences and should not be missed on a Barcelona trip. Also, this area is where many of the city's luxurious restaurants, markets, and hotels are located.

Mexico(nort-america)

i have visited the place in 2016

Overall, Mexico is safe to travel to, and the vast majority of visits go smoothly, without any incidents. Most popular tourist destinations are pretty safe, as long as you take basic precautions. The media picks up and sensationalizes the few cases that go wrong
